Free Feather


We believe that with togetherness, creativity, and initiative we are able to inspire each other, bringing together collaborative activities. Free Feather is a Center of Inspiration in Athens, organising three days a week gatherings with discussions, workshops, missions and jam sessions.

Entrance is by invitation only, free of charge including food and drinks! What is necessary, is your participation.

Recipe

Four homemade ingredients create explosive and creative gatherings. Subjects, Projects, Sessions and Participants. Six Subjects for a variety of Projects and Sessions that engage many Participants to host and join. A feast for creative activities!

Subjects

With a good selection of subjects, we can also have many interesting projects and sessions. That is why it can be easy for many people to be interested in our activities. Art, Technology, Health, Permaculture, Travel and Communication are what we base these activities on.

Projects

A project is a series of sessions that are good to follow through from day one, continuing its purpose and goals. A project usually start at the beginning of a season and lasts until the end. You can get involved with a project at any time.

Sessions

A Session is a one time event that can be part of a project or not. A session can be a discussion, a workshop, a mission or a jam-session. The sessions are the building blocks of a gathering, organized by the core group and hosted by any of the participants.

Participants

For a center of inspiration to function, we invite guests and members to be part of workshops, discussions, celebrations and missions. These events inspire us to be creative together and collaborate in various ways.
